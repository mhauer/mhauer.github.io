Marcus Hauer

@mhauer almost everywhere.

Bona fide entrepreneur. Disciplined designer. Pragmatic consultant. Assorted geek. Liberal foodie. Superstitious footballer. Investing in @thefutureberlin. Previously Director @IDEO.

Marcus is a Berlin-based designer, who is focused on digital products from idea to launch. He learned early on to first and foremost focus on the user and their experience during his time at IDEO in San Francisco. During this time in Silicon Valley he worked for such companies as Amazon, Microsoft, Cisco and Nokia. To his own surprise he’s now listed with two software patents owned by Apple and Microsoft.

Recently he’s been a visiting professor at a university incubator in Germany. While he is a designer at heart, he always enjoyed writing and early in his career ended up at De:Bug Magazine where he launched the design editorial.

Together with Anne Pascual, Marcus co-founded Schoenerwissen/OfCD, a design studio specialized in information design and visualization where he worked on several projects with such luminaries as Bruce Sterling, John Thackara, Lev Manovich and Casey Reas. His work has been featured in various publications, exhibitions and presentations around the world.

Marcus holds a design diploma from Cologne’s Academy of Media Arts and was a researcher at University of California in Santa Barbara.
